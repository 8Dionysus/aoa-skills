# Recurrence review decision closure for skills

Skill beacons often mean either trigger drift or unused-skill opportunity. A decision packet should distinguish a genuine miss from explicit-only restraint, collision avoidance, unavailable host support, or intentional non-use.

Use `suppress` only with a narrow reason and scope. Use `accept` to point at trigger-eval, description, bundle, or applicability-map followthrough rather than silently changing activation behavior.
